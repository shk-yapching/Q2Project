Boracay

Boracay is one of the top islands in the Philippines. It is known for its beaches with powdery white sand, crystal clear waters, and beautiful views.

Boracay is only an hour away from Manila which makes it accessible to many tourists and even for the locals. Here you can swim, go island-hopping, diving, snorkeling, and many more.

Palawan

Palawan is famous for its fantastic beaches. The popular beach towns of Palawan are El Nido and Puerto Princesa wherein you can do different activities. Other than the beautiful beaches of Palawan, you can also make time to explore the mountainous interior and thick jungle.

Siargao

 Despite being a well-kept secret destination, Siargao has slowly become one of the fanous tourist destinations in the Philippines. Siargao is known to be the surfing capital of the Philippines. This is because it is said to be a result of a collusion of two tectonic plates and the deep depths around Siargao, which gave good surf spots around the Island.
